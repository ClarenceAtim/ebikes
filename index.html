About Heybike Sports

Heybike Sports was built by riders, for riders. We set out to make high-performance electric dirt bikes and pit bikes that deliver real motorcycle thrills — without the fuel, the noise, or the maintenance headaches.

Every model is engineered around powerful mid-drive motors, long-range batteries, and full suspension, then tested hard on dirt, trails, and pavement before it ever reaches your garage. From the flagship Villain Combo to our everyday commuter e-bikes, the goal is the same: more range, more torque, and more fun on every ride.

We back it all with responsive U.S.-based support, fast free shipping, and a 1-year warranty — because the ride should be the only thing you have to think about.
